Hi, I'm Mainak Chakraborty, an engineer living in Delhi, India. I'm interested in leveraging AI to improve and build robust cyber-physical systems. My research focus is on end-to-end development for IoT systems, particularly in the seismic sensor.

Currently, I am research scholar at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi

I graduated from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur with an M.Tech in Robotics with concentrations in systems and artificial intelligence. At IIEST, my research was supported by the GATE scholarship. After that, I joined the Robotics and Automation Department of CSIR-CMERI as a research Assistant from 2020 to 2021.
