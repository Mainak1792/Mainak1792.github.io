Hello! I’m Dr. (in progress) Mainak Chakraborty, a PhD candidate in Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi. I am currently advised by Prof. Subrat Kar. My doctoral research is supported by the Prime Minister Research Fellowship (PMRF), focusing on TinyML and cyber-physical systems.

Prior to my PhD, I earned an M.Tech in Mechatronics from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST), Shibpur, backed by a GATE scholarship. I also spent time at the Robotics and Automation Department of CSIR-CMERI, where I worked as a Research Assistant from 2020 to 2021. Additionally, I co-founded a startup etaaide in 2022. This site is my academic home on the web 🚀.
