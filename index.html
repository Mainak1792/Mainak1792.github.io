Hi, I'm Mainak Chakraborty, an engineer living in New Delhi, India. I'm interested in leveraging AI to improve and build robust cyber-physical systems. My research focus is on user and user-behaviour identification using sensor.

Currently, I am a Research Scholar at Indian Institute of Technology(IIT), Delhi. My Research at IIT-Delhi is supported by Prime Minister Research Fellowship (PMRF).

I graduated from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology(IIEST), Shibpur with an M.Tech in Mechatronics. At IIEST, my research was supported by the GATE scholarship. After that, I joined the Robotics and Automation Department of CSIR-CMERI as a research Assistant from 2020 to 2021. This is my home on the internet 🚀.
